All equipment is carefully inspected before shipping. If you receive an item that doesn't meet our quality standards, we'll make it right.

  1. Contact us at iskoolsports@gmail.com within 3 days of delivery
  2. Include your order number and reason for return/exchange
  3. We'll provide a prepaid return shipping label
  4. Package the item securely and ship it back
  5. Once received, we'll process your refund or send your exchange

Note: Items must be in original condition with all original packaging and tags.
